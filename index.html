Our tools

Each tool handles a specific part of the compliance process — together, they create a complete workflow for healthcare onboarding.

NMC Check

NMC validation

  • Validate NMC registration status, expiry, and practitioner details
  • Process multiple candidates in a structured workflow
  • Generate consistent, easy-to-review outputs for compliance teams
DBS Check

DBS Check Workflow

  • Interpret DBS certificate results with structured output
  • Support document-based verification and record consistency
  • Reduce repeated manual checks across systems
Right to Work

Right to Work Verification

  • Interpret right to work status, visa conditions, and expiry
  • Highlight restrictions and compliance requirements clearly
  • Produce audit-ready outputs for employer records
Checklist

Onboarding Profile Checklists

  • Extract and organise candidate compliance data automatically
  • Populate onboarding profiles and compliance templates
  • Reduce repetitive form completion and manual review effort
