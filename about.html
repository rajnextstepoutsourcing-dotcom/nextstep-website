About NextStep Compliance

Built for the people who keep healthcare safe

NextStep was built by people who understood the compliance pain of UK healthcare recruitment first-hand — and decided to fix it properly.

Our Mission

Compliance shouldn't slow you down

Healthcare recruitment agencies spend a staggering amount of time on manual compliance checks — NMC verifications, DBS reviews, Right to Work confirmations. Hours spent on tasks that could be automated.

We built NextStep Compliance to give that time back. Every tool we build is designed around a single principle: the check should be faster than the conversation about doing it.

Our platform is used by healthcare staffing agencies across the UK to process hundreds of compliance checks daily — accurately, securely, and without the admin burden.

"The compliance team at a typical mid-sized healthcare agency spends 15–20 hours per week on manual verification tasks that our platform handles in minutes."
— NextStep Compliance founding observation
