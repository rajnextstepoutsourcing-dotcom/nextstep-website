Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2026  ·  NextStep Compliance, Registered in England & Wales

Summary: We collect only what we need to provide our service. We never sell your data or candidate data. All data is stored securely in the UK. You have full rights over your data under UK GDPR.

1. Who We Are

NextStep Compliance ("NextStep", "we", "us", "our") is a UK-based software company providing compliance automation tools for healthcare recruitment agencies. We are the data controller for the personal data we process in connection with our platform.

For any data protection queries, contact us at: privacy@nextstep.co.uk

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

  • Account data: your name, company name, email address, phone number, and password (hashed — never stored in plain text).
  • Candidate data: name, date of birth, DBS certificate number, NMC PIN, share code, and other compliance-relevant details submitted by your team for verification purposes.
  • Usage data: which tools you use, number of checks run, job submission timestamps, and token consumption records.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, and access logs for security and fraud prevention purposes.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and operate the NextStep platform and its tools.
  • To process compliance verification requests submitted by your team.
  • To manage your subscription, tokens, and billing.
  • To send service-related communications such as account confirmations and usage alerts.
  • To maintain platform security and detect fraud or abuse.
  • To comply with our legal obligations under UK law.

We do not use your data or candidate data for marketing purposes, profiling, or any purpose not directly connected to delivering our service.

4. Candidate Data — Special Obligations

Our platform processes personal data about third-party individuals (candidates submitted by your agency). Your agency acts as the data controller for candidate data. NextStep acts as your data processor.

You are responsible for ensuring you have a lawful basis to submit candidate data to our platform and that candidates have been made aware of the processing where required by UK GDPR.

Candidate data is processed solely to fulfil the specific verification request. It is never shared with other customers, never used for any other purpose, and is isolated strictly to your company account.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored on UK-based or UK-compliant infrastructure.
  • Data is isolated per customer — no cross-tenant data access is technically possible.
  • All connections are encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms — we cannot read your password.
  • Access to data is role-controlled within your organisation.
  • Output files are stored in isolated per-job storage paths and are only accessible by authorised users in your account.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your subscription is active and for a reasonable period thereafter as required for legal or legitimate business purposes.

Job outputs and candidate data are retained for a period agreed at account setup or for a default period of 90 days after job completion, after which they are securely deleted.

You may request early deletion of any data by contacting us at privacy@nextstep.co.uk.

7. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

As a data subject, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restrict or object to processing of your data.
  • Data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, contact privacy@nextstep.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Third-Party Services

We use a small number of trusted third-party services to operate our platform. These include hosting infrastructure and form submission services. We do not share personal data with advertising networks, data brokers, or analytics platforms.

Any third-party processors we use are bound by data processing agreements and UK GDPR obligations.

9. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for session management and platform security. We do not use tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect platform functionality.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify active customers of material changes by email. The most current version will always be available at this URL.

11. Contact and Complaints

For any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, contact us at privacy@nextstep.co.uk.

If you believe we have not handled your data correctly, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.
