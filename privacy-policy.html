Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026. This draft is written for a UK-focused healthcare compliance platform and should be reviewed again once your domain, company address and business email are finalised.

1. Who we are

NextStep Compliance is a healthcare compliance automation platform operated by NextStep Outsourcing. Until the final domain and business email are in place, account and platform queries may be directed to raj.nextstepoutsourcing@gmail.com.

2. What data we collect

  • Account details such as name, company, email address and phone number.
  • Usage information such as login sessions, jobs run, tools used and token consumption.
  • Documents and candidate-related information uploaded for checklist or verification workflows.

3. Why we collect it

  • To provide access to the platform and manage approvals.
  • To run healthcare compliance workflows requested by your organisation.
  • To maintain records of usage, security events and service administration.

4. Temporary storage and retention

The platform is designed so temporary output files can be deleted after download rather than stored permanently. Operational records such as job history, account details and usage logs may remain in the database for administration, support, security and billing purposes.

5. Legal basis

Depending on the context, processing may be based on contract, legitimate interests, legal obligations or consent where required. Organisations using the platform remain responsible for ensuring they have an appropriate basis for submitting personal data for processing.

6. Sharing

We may use infrastructure and software providers to host the service, such as hosting, database, email or workflow service providers. We do not sell personal data.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect data, including authenticated access, session controls and ownership checks within the platform workflow. No internet-based system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Your rights

If UK GDPR applies, you may have rights including access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection and complaint to the Information Commissioner's Office where relevant.

9. Contact

For now, privacy-related contact can be directed to raj.nextstepoutsourcing@gmail.com. Replace this with your final business email before launch.
